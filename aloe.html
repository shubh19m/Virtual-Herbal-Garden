Aloe Vera

Aloe vera (Aloe barbadensis miller) is widely used for its medicinal and cosmetic benefits.

  • Skin Care:Aloe vera gel soothes burns, wounds, and cuts, and is used to treat acne, psoriasis, and eczema.
  • Digestive Health:Consuming aloe vera juice can help with constipation, acid reflux, and irritable bowel syndrome (IBS).
  • Hydration: Aloe vera is rich in water content, making it excellent for skin hydration and as a natural moisturizer.
  • Wound Healing: Its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties accelerate wound healing.
  • detoxification: Aloe vera helps detoxify the body by aiding liver function and promoting healthy digestion.

Medical Uses

  • Gynecological Health: Ashoka bark is widely used in Ayurvedic medicine for treating various gynecological disorders like excessive uterine bleeding, irregular periods, and menstrual pain.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Its compounds possess anti-inflammatory properties, useful in reducing swelling and pain.
  • Skin Health: Applied topically for skin conditions due to its purifying properties.
  • Digestive Aid: Helps in treating indigestion and diarrhea.
  • Antioxidant Properties: Contains antioxidants that protect cells from damage.
